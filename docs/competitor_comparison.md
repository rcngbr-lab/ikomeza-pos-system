# Competitor Comparison

## Odoo POS

Odoo is stronger in ERP breadth, accounting, apps, and ecosystem. IKOMEZA can compete on local simplicity, faster onboarding, Rwanda payment workflows, and restaurant/bar department focus.

## Loyverse

Loyverse is polished and easy for small retail. IKOMEZA can compete where businesses need local ownership, custom workflows, branch control, and deeper restaurant/store accountability.

## Aronium

Aronium is strong for Windows desktop retail. IKOMEZA can compete with web/mobile access, Laravel customization, multi-department restaurant flows, and cloud deployment.

## What IKOMEZA Must Not Overclaim

- Certified tax device integration.
- Fully automated offline sync.
- Mature accounting replacement.
- 100-branch scale without PostgreSQL/MySQL, queues, monitoring, and load testing.
