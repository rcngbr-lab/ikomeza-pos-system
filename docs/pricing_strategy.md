# Pricing Strategy

## Recommended Pilot Pricing

- Starter: one branch, POS, products, sales, shifts, basic reports.
- Restaurant/Bar: Starter plus tables, KOT/BOT, departments, stock controls.
- Enterprise: multi-branch, audit, backup, reconciliation, advanced reports, support SLA.

## What Not To Claim Yet

- Do not claim certified RRA EBM integration until a real EBM/CIS/SDC vendor integration is implemented and certified.
- Do not claim offline multi-branch sync is complete; the durable foundation exists, but conflict UI and background workers still need pilot hardening.
- Do not claim accounting-suite replacement; journal/ledger foundation exists, but accounting exports and reconciliations need more coverage.

## Pricing Principle

Charge for implementation, training, data migration, support, and backups separately from software subscription.
