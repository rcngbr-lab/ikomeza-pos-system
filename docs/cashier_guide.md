# Cashier Guide

## Start Shift

Open your shift before selling. The system uses the active shift to track sales, expected cash, payment mix, and shortages.

## Selling

1. Open POS.
2. Search, scan, or tap product.
3. Adjust quantity.
4. Select payment method.
5. For MoMo, Airtel, bank, or card, enter reference or transaction ID.
6. Complete sale.
7. Print receipt.

## Not Allowed

- Do not use another branch account.
- Do not refund without approval if approval is required.
- Do not enter fake references for mobile money/card/bank payments.
