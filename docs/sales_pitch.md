# Sales Pitch

## Positioning

IKOMEZA POS is a Rwanda-focused POS and operations control platform for restaurants, bars, shops, and supermarkets that need sales, stock, shifts, departments, and accountability in one system.

## Differentiators

- Username-based staff login for local daily operations.
- Kitchen/Bar department separation with one POS receipt.
- Branch-aware access control foundation.
- Payment reconciliation for MoMo, Airtel, bank, and card references.
- Backup/restore commands and admin backup screen.
- Audit trail protection for accountability.

## Honest Pilot Message

IKOMEZA POS is ready for controlled pilot customers after configuration and training. It should not yet be sold as a fully certified national-scale enterprise ERP or RRA EBM-certified fiscal system.
